FloraFacts

Lavender

Lavender has a long history of medicinal use. It has been traditionally used to alleviate headaches, ease digestive issues, relieve anxiety, and soothe skin irritations.

Tulip

Tulips became extremely popular in the 17th century in the Netherlands during a period known as "Tulip Mania."

Rose

Roses have been used for various purposes throughout history. They have been used in perfumes, cosmetics, and culinary creations. Rose water, made by distilling rose petals, is often used in cooking and beauty products.

Sunflowers

Sunflowers are highly beneficial to the environment. They have deep root systems that help improve soil quality and prevent erosion. They are also attractive to pollinators and provide food and shelter for birds and other wildlife.

Lotus

The lotus is known for its ability to grow in muddy or stagnant waters while maintaining its purity and beauty. This characteristic is often seen as a metaphor for spiritual growth and transformation.

Lily

Some species of lilies are toxic to cats, so it's important to keep them out of reach of feline companions.
